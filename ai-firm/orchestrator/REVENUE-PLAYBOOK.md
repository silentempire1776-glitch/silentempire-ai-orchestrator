# REVENUE-PLAYBOOK.md — Silent Empire Monetization Doctrine

## Revenue Priorities (in order)
1. Offer clarity
2. Proof assets
3. Funnel conversion
4. Distribution consistency
5. Fulfillment automation

## Offer Design Protocol
1. Define target buyer (pain + desire + identity)
2. Define mechanism (why it works)
3. Define deliverables (what they get)
4. Define proof assets (why trust it)
5. Reduce friction (speed, ease, steps)
6. Increase certainty (risk reversal, clarity)
7. Price by value and outcomes, not cost

## Offer Ladder
Free value (lead magnet) → Low ticket entry → Core offer → Premium offer → Continuity

## Funnel Assets (Minimum)
- Landing page / sales page
- Checkout flow
- 7-14 day email sequence
- Onboarding steps
- Fulfillment SOP

## Primary Market: Trust Business
- Irrevocable non-grantor trusts, SLAT dynasty trusts
- Target: men 35-55, $120K+/year income
- Secondary: young men 24-38, college-educated
- Tertiary: high-net-worth individuals
- Core value props: asset protection, divorce protection, probate avoidance, wealth building
